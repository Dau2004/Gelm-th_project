📋 INSTALLATION STEPS

1. ENABLE INSTALLATION FROM UNKNOWN SOURCES
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   • Open "Settings" on your Android phone
   • Go to "Security" or "Privacy"
   • Find "Install unknown apps" or "Unknown sources"
   • Enable it for your file manager or browser

2. TRANSFER THE APP FILE
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   • Copy "gelmath-cmam-v1.0.0.apk" to your phone
   • You can use:
     - USB cable
     - WhatsApp/Email
     - Bluetooth
     - SD card

3. INSTALL THE APP
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   • Locate the APK file on your phone
   • Tap on "gelmath-cmam-v1.0.0.apk"
   • Tap "Install"
   • Wait for installation to complete
   • Tap "Open"

4. FIRST TIME LOGIN
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   Use your assigned credentials:
   
   • CHW (Community Health Worker):
     Username: chw_user
     Password: chw123
   
   • Doctor:
     Username: doctor_user
     Password: doctor123
   
   • MoH Admin:
     Username: moh_admin
     Password: admin123

🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem: "App not installed"
Solution: Uninstall any old version first, then reinstall

Problem: "Installation blocked"
Solution: Enable "Unknown sources" in Settings → Security

Problem: "Parse error"
Solution: Re-download the APK file, ensure it's not corrupted

Problem: "Cannot login"
Solution: Check internet connection, verify credentials

Problem: App crashes
Solution: Clear app data in Settings → Apps → Gelmath CMAM

📖 QUICK START GUIDE

1. Open the app
2. Login with your credentials
3. Tap "New Assessment"
4. Fill in child information:
   - Child ID
   - Sex (Male/Female)
   - Age (6-59 months)
   - MUAC measurement (mm)
   - Edema grade (0-3)
   - Appetite (Good/Poor)
   - Danger signs
5. Tap "Submit"
6. View results and recommended care pathway
7. Save assessment
8. Sync when online

🎯 CARE PATHWAYS

The app recommends one of these pathways:

• SC-ITP: Stabilization Center / Inpatient Therapeutic Program
  (Severe Acute Malnutrition with complications)

• OTP: Outpatient Therapeutic Program
  (Severe Acute Malnutrition without complications)

• TSFP: Targeted Supplementary Feeding Program
  (Moderate Acute Malnutrition)
